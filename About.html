About K.E.E.A STEM

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team behind our STEM initiatives

Who We Are

K.E.E.A STEM is a non-profit organization established in 2018 with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between traditional education and the evolving demands of the 21st century. We believe that every student deserves access to quality STEM education that not only teaches technical skills but also fosters creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

Based in the KEEA District of Ghana's Central Region, we have grown from a small community initiative to a recognized leader in STEM education, serving schools and communities across the region. Our innovative approach combines hands-on learning with theoretical knowledge, creating an engaging and effective educational experience.

Our Mission

To empower students through innovative STEM education programs that develop critical thinking, creativity, and technical skills necessary for success in the modern world. We strive to make STEM education accessible, engaging, and relevant to all students, regardless of their background.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for STEM education transformation in West Africa, creating a generation of innovative thinkers and problem-solvers who will drive technological advancement and economic growth in their communities and beyond.

Our Values

  • Innovation in education delivery
  • Inclusivity and equal access
  • Excellence in all our programs
  • Community collaboration
  • Sustainable development
